Use the arrows of your keyboard to move your yoshi

You can move in the menus with the arrow `up` and `down`, change the values with `left` and `right`

Press Enter to change menu (example : If you're in the option menu, go on 'Back' and press Enter)

The body of Yoshi represents the hitbox ;)

Easy

Max Number of bonus/malus : 4

Goomba : -5 points (50% chance of appearance)

Star : 10 points (50% chance of appearance)

After takin 5 stars without Goomba, you have a 10-point bonus

Medium

Max Number of bonus/malus : 8

Goomba : -5 points (40% chance of appearance)

Star : 10 points (40% chance of appearance)

Fire : It kills you (20% chance of appearance)

After takin 5 stars without Goomba, you have a 20-point bonus

There are walls ! One advice, avoid it or you will die !

Hard

Max Number of bonus/malus : 12

Goomba : -5 points (40% chance of appearance)

Star : 10 points (30% chance of appearance)

Fire : It kills you (30% chance of appearance)

After takin 5 stars without Goomba, you have a 30-point bonus

There are MORE walls ! One advice, avoid it or you will die !
